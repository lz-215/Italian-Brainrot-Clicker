How to Play

To play Fish Frenzy, use your mouse or touch controls to move your net and catch as many fish as possible. The more fish you catch in Fish Frenzy, the higher your score. Watch out for special fish and power-ups!

Use the left mouse button or WASD or arrow keys to move.
